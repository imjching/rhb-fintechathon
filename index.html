How it works
It's quite easy to understand.

  1. Snap a photo of your purchase.
  2. Tag your photo with details such as price, category, and location.
  3. Post your photo. (It will be instantly shared with all your friends!)
  4. For every interaction with the app, not only do you get to receive SnapBudget points (SB), you'd be able to stand a chance to gain up to double the points* if you're the least spender of the week (among your friends who played)!
  5. Gain points for rewards!

* Formula is based on the number of active friends you have.
